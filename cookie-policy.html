Cookie Policy - Real Estate Agency Website

On our real estate agency website, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. To enhance the experience of our visitors and clients, we use cookies on our website. This cookie policy aims to explain what cookies are, how we use them on our site, the types of cookies we utilize, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or any other internet-enabled device) when you visit a website. These files contain information that allows the website to remember your activities or preferences over time. Cookies are commonly used to improve website functionality, increase efficiency, and provide a personalized user experience.

There are different types of cookies used for various purposes. Some cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website, while others help track user behavior to improve the site or for advertising purposes.

2. Why do we use Cookies on our Real Estate Website?

On our real estate website, we use cookies for several reasons. Here are some of the main purposes for using cookies on our platform:

  • Enhancing User Experience: Cookies allow us to save your preferences and settings so you don’t have to re-enter the same information each time you visit our website.
  • Personalization: Cookies enable us to offer you personalized property listings and relevant content based on your browsing behavior on our website.
  • Analytics and Performance: We use cookies to gather data on how users interact with our website, which helps us improve its functionality and design.
  • Advertising: Cookies help us show you relevant ads related to real estate services and property offers, based on your activity on our site.
  • Security: Some cookies are used to ensure the security of our website, such as verifying user identity and protecting against fraud.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies on our website. Below are the main types of cookies we employ:

3.1. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are critical for the proper operation of our website. Without these cookies, some parts of the site may not be accessible or function correctly. These cookies do not require your consent and are automatically enabled when you visit our website.

Examples of essential cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your activities during your session, such as keeping you logged in as you navigate between pages.
  • Security Cookies: We use cookies to verify that a user is authorized to access certain areas of the website, such as personal account pages or contact forms.

3.2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us analyze how users interact with our real estate website. This data helps us improve website performance and understand which areas need enhancement. Performance cookies collect information anonymously and are not linked to personal data.

Examples of performance cookies we use:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics cookies to collect data on website traffic and user behavior, such as the most viewed property listings and average time spent on pages. This information helps us optimize the user experience.

3.3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable the website to remember your selections and preferences, such as your preferred language or search filters for properties. These cookies improve the personalization of your experience on our website.

Examples of functional cookies we use:

  • Language Preferences: We use cookies to store your preferred language so you don’t have to select it every time you visit our site.
  • Search Filters: We use cookies to save the filters and criteria you set, such as property type, price range, or location, to make your property search more efficient.

3.4. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to show you personalized ads based on your browsing behavior. These cookies allow us to present relevant advertisements related to real estate services, either on our website or on partner sites.

Examples of advertising cookies we use:

  • Google Ads: We use Google Ads cookies to display personalized ads based on your activity on our website and your browsing behavior on other sites.
  • Facebook Pixel: We use Facebook Pixel cookies to show ads related to our real estate listings on the Facebook platform.

3.5. Social Media Cookies

Social media cookies enable integration between our website and social networks like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn. These cookies allow you to share content directly from our site and help us measure user interaction with our social media profiles.

Examples of social media cookies we use:

  • Facebook: We use Facebook cookies to show personalized ads and allow users to share content on their Facebook profiles.
  • Twitter: We use Twitter cookies to enable sharing on Twitter and track interactions with our Twitter profile.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You can disable or block cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may limit some functionalities on our website or cause parts of the site to not work properly.

Below is how you can manage cookies in the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy and Security" > "Site Settings" > "Cookies" and adjust your preferences.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Go to "Options" > "Privacy & Security" > "Cookies and Site Data" and customize your settings.
  • Safari: Go to "Preferences" > "Privacy" and manage cookie options.
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy, Search, and Services" > "Cookies and Site Permissions" and modify your preferences.

5. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites that we do not operate or control. This cookie policy applies solely to our site, so we recommend reviewing the cookie policies of any other websites you visit.

6. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this cookie policy at any time. Any changes will be published on this page, so we encourage you to review our policy regularly.
