Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Protecting your privacy is very important to us.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, protect, and use personal information ("Personal Data") you ("User," "you," or "your") provide on our real estate website and in connection with our real estate services ("Website" or "Services"). It also explains your options regarding the use of your Personal Data and how you can access and update it. This Policy does not apply to practices of third parties we do not control or employ.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and store any information you voluntarily provide when you fill out forms on our Website, such as contact details for inquiries about properties, scheduling viewings, or registering for newsletters. If you choose not to provide certain information, you may not be able to use all features of our Website or Services.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you visit our Website, our servers automatically record information your browser sends, including your device's IP address, browser type and version, operating system type and version, preferred language, referring webpage, pages you visit on our Website, time spent on these pages, search queries, access times and dates, and other usage statistics.

4. Use of Collected Information

The information collected helps us respond to your inquiries about properties, manage property listings, improve our real estate services, and operate the Website efficiently. Non-personal data is used to identify potential misuse and compile anonymous statistical reports on Website usage without identifying individual users.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to gather information for statistical purposes and to operate and enhance our Website and real estate services. You can choose to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers accept cookies by default, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if preferred.

6. Advertising and Data Collection

Our Website may use advertising services such as Google Ads to display relevant property advertisements. These services use cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to our Website or other sites. Advertising providers may share data across their services to personalize your ad experience. You can disable personalized ads by visiting the Google Ads settings page or opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

7. Data Security

We protect the data you provide on secure servers with restricted access to prevent unauthorized use or disclosure. We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of personal data under our control.

8. Links to External Websites

Our Website may contain links to external real estate-related sites not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing this Policy periodically.
